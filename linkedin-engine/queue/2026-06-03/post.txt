Most ad accounts don't have a spending problem.
They have a hook problem.

We audited 40+ ecom accounts this year. The pattern was almost boring:

→ ~90% of budget flows to ads people decide on in the first 3 seconds.
→ But ~90% of production time goes into the other 27.

The first frame IS the ad. Everything after is for people you've already won.

What actually moved hook rate for us:

• Open on motion or a face — never a logo
• Put the claim in text on frame 1, don't make them listen for it
• Test 5 hooks against 1 body, not 5 full videos
• Trim the first 0.5s after export (that "settle" frame quietly kills retention)

The cheapest lever in your account isn't a new audience.
It's a better first second.
